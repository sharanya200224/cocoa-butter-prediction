Product Recommendations

Medium Quality Recommendations

This cocoa butter is of acceptable quality but requires refining for premium applications. The elevated viscosity and oleic acid levels indicate it would benefit from additional processing.

Recommended Refining Process

  • Filtration: Use activated carbon filtration to remove impurities and contaminants
  • Deodorization: Steam deodorization to eliminate any unwanted odors
  • Fractionation: Separate triglycerides to improve consistency
  • Winterization: Remove high-melting triglycerides to improve texture
  • Additives: Consider adding tocopherols to improve stability
