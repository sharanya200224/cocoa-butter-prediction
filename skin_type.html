Skincare Tips

For Dry Skin

  • Look for cocoa butter rich in SOS and Oleic Acid for deep hydration
  • Avoid unrefined types if you have sensitivity to natural cocoa scents
  • Combine with hyaluronic acid-based serum for enhanced moisture retention
  • Apply immediately after showering to lock in moisture
  • Use thicker formulations at night for intensive repair

For Oily Skin

  • Choose cocoa butter with higher POP/POS ratios for lighter texture
  • Look for non-comedogenic formulations that won't clog pores
  • Use sparingly and focus on dry areas only
  • Combine with salicylic acid for better oil control
  • Avoid heavy applications during humid conditions

For Sensitive Skin

  • Opt for refined cocoa butter to reduce potential irritants
  • Look for products with high Stearic Acid for barrier protection
  • Patch test before full application
  • Combine with calming ingredients like chamomile or oat extract
  • Avoid products with added fragrances or preservatives

How Cocoa Butter Works

Fatty Acids

Stearic and palmitic acids form a protective barrier that locks in moisture and shields skin from environmental stressors.

Triglycerides

SOS triglycerides create a moisture-locking matrix that prevents dehydration and maintains skin elasticity.

Antioxidants

Natural polyphenols in cocoa butter neutralize free radicals and reduce signs of aging.
